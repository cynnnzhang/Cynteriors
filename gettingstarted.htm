WHAT YOU'll NEED

PlanningWiz

Planning Wiz is a free online planner software that will allow you to create 3D floor and room plans.

Photoshop

Photoshop is a great tool to invest in to create mood boards for your rooms. Mood boards are essential as they allow you to visualize how your room is put together.
