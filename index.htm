WELCOME

Hi! I’m Cynthia and this website is for other aspiring or hobbying interior designers who want to learn how to use design software to their best advantage!

On a low budget and lack resources such as a room to decorate, the items that will fill the space, or the tools needed to perform? Don’t worry! This website will teach you how to use resources like floor plan software and mood board creators on a tight budget right in your home!

What will you learn?

This website will take you through the step by step process to creating a room with a beautiful interior! I’ll take you through creating a simple bedroom, and soon you will be able to create different rooms yourself.
