MOODBOARDING

KEY IDEA: Visualising your Space

Moodboards are made up of different images, materials, and text, and are a great way to visualize the concept and style of your room. For those looking ahead to a career in interior design, mood boards are especially great to convey your project to a client.

ELEMENTS

I love how interior design can embody feelings brought to you by your surroundings. Everyone's different and I encourage you to design your living spaces to fit the vibe you want to feel at home. Moodboards really aid in pulling together all the elements to create a cohesive vibe.

COLOUR PALETTE

This is a simple one. Warm or cool? Neutral or bold? Primary or secondary? Complimenting or contrasting?

ACCENTS

Accents bring uniqueness and boldness to a space. I personally like a pop of a bright color or a rough texture to bring personality into rooms. Shapes, textures, colour, and lighting can all be accents that will bring dynamism into a room.

TEXTURES AND TEXTILES

Textures can be a part of your accents but also add another layer to plain hues. Adding textiles like faux leather, faux fur, wood, glass, ceramic, and metal add personality to a space.

SHAPES

Another accent, but can also be used throughout. Fun, swirly shapes contribute to a unique look, straight edges to a minimalist look, patterns and mandalas to a boho look, and triangles for an edgy look.
